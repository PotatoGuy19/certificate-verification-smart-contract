Upload Your Certificates to the Blockchain

What is this?

This web application allows you to upload your education certificate to a permanent, decentralized storage system called IPFS (Inter-Planetary File System). It then stores your signature on an Ethereum smart contract to verify the document's authenticity in the future.

Click "Submit" to send your certificate to decentralized storage. The application will return the certificate's IPFS address. Meanwhile, it will write a signature to Ethereum for you.

Click "Verify" to view your signature on the Ethereum blockchain. The application will tell you who is the issuer and the recipient as long as you present the correct IPFS hash address.
